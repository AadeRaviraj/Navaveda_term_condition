Privacy Policy for Navaveda

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Navaveda ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as name, email address, and profile photo
  • App usage data including study sessions, quiz attempts, and interactions
  • AI chat inputs and responses to improve user experience

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Track your progress and performance
  • Enable AI-powered features
  • Send notifications (if enabled)

Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using third-party services such as Firebase, Supabase (PostgreSQL), and Cloudinary. We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication – user login
  • Firebase Realtime Database – user data and chat history
  • Supabase – course content and structured data
  • Cloudinary – image storage
  • Groq – AI processing

These services have their own privacy policies.

Data Retention & Automatic Deletion

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active.

Inactive Account Policy:
If your account shows no activity (such as no login, no study session, or no quiz attempt) for a continuous period of 60 days, we may remove certain inactive data to optimize storage.

This may include deletion of:

  • Study session history
  • Quiz attempt records

Note:
Your core account data, including profile information and AI chat history, will not be deleted as part of this inactivity policy.

Manual Deletion:
You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at raviraj.s.aade@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

Your Rights

You may have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data depending on your location.

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact Us
